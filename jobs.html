Working at Etihad Airways

Etihad Airways is the national airline of the United Arab Emirates, operating flights to over 100 destinations worldwide. The airline regularly hires professionals from different countries, offering opportunities in cabin crew, ground operations, engineering, and corporate roles.

This page provides a **clear overview of Etihad job opportunities** and how to apply safely through official channels.

Popular Role

Cabin Crew (Flight Attendant)

Cabin crew members are responsible for passenger safety, comfort, and service during flights. Etihad looks for individuals who are professional, customer-focused, and able to work in an international environment.

  • Minimum age requirement (varies by recruitment cycle)
  • Good communication skills in English
  • Customer service or hospitality experience is an advantage
  • Willingness to relocate to Abu Dhabi
