Online Skills

Start Here (Beginner Friendly)

These guides are educational. We do not promise income results. Your success depends on skill, effort, consistency, and market demand.

1) Copywriting (Writing that sells)

  • Learn how to write clear headlines and short offers
  • Practice writing product descriptions daily
  • Build a small portfolio (Google Docs or Notion)

2) Content Creation (TikTok / YouTube)

  • Pick one topic: jobs, skills, motivation, or stories
  • Post consistently (even 3x a week)
  • Focus on clarity, not perfection

3) Affiliate Marketing (Basics)

  • Understand: you recommend products and may earn commission
  • Start with helpful content (reviews, tutorials, comparisons)
  • Avoid “get rich quick” promises—focus on value
