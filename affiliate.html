Basics

What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is when you recommend a product or service using a special tracking link. If someone buys through your link, the company may pay you a commission.

⚠️ Results are not guaranteed. Success depends on trust, traffic, consistency, and time.
How it Works

The Simple Process

  • Join an affiliate program
  • Get a unique affiliate link
  • Create helpful content (blog/video/post)
  • Share your link naturally inside the content
  • Earn commission if someone buys through your link
Step 1

Pick a Niche (Topic)

Choose a topic you can stick to for months:

  • Online work & digital skills
  • Phones / gadgets / tech
  • Education & courses
  • Travel (booking tools, insurance)
  • Fitness (be honest, no false health claims)
Step 2

Choose Products to Promote

  • Promote what you understand
  • Be honest about pros and cons
  • Don’t promote scammy “get rich quick” products
  • Prefer brands with good support and refunds
Step 3

Create Helpful Content (The Real Secret)

People buy when they trust you. Create content like:

  • “Best tools for beginners”
  • “How to use X step-by-step”
  • “X vs Y — which is better and why?”
  • “Mistakes beginners make with X”
30-Day Plan

Beginner 30-Day Plan

  • Days 1–3: Learn terms + choose niche
  • Days 4–10: Find products + understand them
  • Days 11–20: Write 3 helpful posts
  • Days 21–30: Improve, rewrite, and be consistent
Avoid

Common Mistakes

  • Spamming affiliate links everywhere
  • Copying other people’s articles word-for-word
  • Promising guaranteed income
  • Promoting a product you don’t understand
Final Advice

Simple Rule

Help first. Sell second. That’s how affiliate marketing lasts long.
