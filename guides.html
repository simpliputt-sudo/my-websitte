How to Apply for Jobs Safely Online

This guide is educational. Always verify details from official sources.

  1. Verify the source: Apply on official company websites or trusted portals.
  2. Never pay money: Legit jobs do not require “processing fees”.
  3. Use a clean CV: One-page if possible, clear contact, clear skills.
  4. Beware of suspicious emails: Poor grammar, urgent threats, or requests for OTP.
  5. Keep records: Save links and screenshots of what you applied for.

Beginner Checklist for Learning Digital Skills

  1. Pick one skill (copywriting, content creation, or basic design).
  2. Learn 30 minutes daily for 2 weeks.
  3. Publish your practice (portfolio, posts, or samples).
  4. Improve using feedback.
  5. Repeat—consistency wins.
